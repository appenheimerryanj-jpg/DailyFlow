## Full description
DailyFlow helps you organize your everyday life without clutter. Plan today’s tasks, track small habits, and capture quick notes in one clean app.

Free features include a focused starter setup for daily use. Upgrade to DailyFlow Premium with a one-time in-app purchase to unlock unlimited tasks, habits, and notes.

Features:
- Daily task list
- Habit streak tracking
- Quick notes
- Clean, simple design
- Offline local storage
- One-time Premium unlock

DailyFlow is built for people who want a simple everyday productivity tool without complicated menus.
